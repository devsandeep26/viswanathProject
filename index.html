About Us

Viswanath Enterprises is the foremost importer , supplier and service provider for superior eminence variety of Dye , Eco solvent, Sublimation inks , UV Inks, Printers , Medias & Displays

Way back in 2007 , Viswanath Enterprises started providing IT sales and services to the corporate as well as SMEs in all business segments. After providing fantastic service and having served many clients, we in 2009 joined hands with Vivid Inks and started providing inks, Spare parts and support for HP wide format printers.

We have intensive experience in handling IT related services since more than 12 years and wide format.Within 2 years of start-up, Viswanath Enterprises has grabbed 70% of the market for inks used for HP & Roland printers.

Viswanath Enterprises has an experience in providing service for more than 250+ customers in Karnataka , Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Tamilnadu and kerala .
